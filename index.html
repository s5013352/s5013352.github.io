Welcome to Candybox F

This is a fork of the original Candybox game, re-written to be played with a Fitbit.

Every step recorded by your fitbit equals one candy (the games unit of currency),

So to win the game your goin to need to take alot of steps.

To get started just click the button below and login to your fitbit account

For best results make sure you are reguarly syncing your fitbit (otherwise the game wont know about your steps)
